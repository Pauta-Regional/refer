The Favorites Menu

MDI Browser can save the address of your favorite pages so you can return to them in the future.

To add a page to your favorites list, select Add to favorites

To make the current page your Home page, select Make this your home.

Your favorites are listed at the end of the menu. To go to one, select it from the menu.

There is no facility to import Favorites from Internet Explorer, or to creates directories of favorites, or to delete or rename your favorites.

If you right click a child window, and select Add to Favorites from the menu, that web page will be saved with your Internet Explorer favorites, and not your MDI Browser favorites.
